2017 Autumn Ridge HOA Night with the Bulls

When: Thursday July 6th. Food starts at 6 pm. Game starts at 7:05pm

Where: Durham Bulls Athletic Park-Budweiser Party Deck

Cost: $5 per Autumn Ridge Resident/Children under 5 free!

RSVP by June 27th to allow for final food and beverage arrangements

All you can eat menu:

  • Bright Leaf hotdogs
  • Hamburgers
  • Grilled chicken
  • Macaroni and cheese
  • Cole slaw
  • Chips and dip
  • Pepsi products and water

Please contact WMD at kherring@wmdouglas.com or by dialing 919-459-1860 to arrange payment.
